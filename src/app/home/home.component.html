No Borders

Founded in late October 2018 by a team of adventurers, No Borders was created for the ones with a love for travel, adventure, and experiencing the new and unique.
Whether your dream is scaling the Alps, strolling through the streets of Rome, or sipping a fresh cup of steaming coffee at a cafe in Paris, you have come to the right place.
Here, with the click of a button, you can begin your adventure and start to explore the world.


Simply sign in and create a bucketlist so you can plan out your dream vacations.
Once visited, add locations to your completed list and keep track of everywhere you have traveled.
